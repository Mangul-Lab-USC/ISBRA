Submission guidelines and publication information

ISBRA 2023 invites submissions to the following tracks via the online submission system (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=isbra2023). Submission implies willingness of at least one author to register and present at the symposium.

Notice: All listed journals are unconfirmed and pending approval for 2023.

Track 1

Submit up to 12 pages in LNCS format for:

  • Presentation at the conference
  • Publication of the extended abstract in the Springer LNBI proceedings
  • Invitation to submit a 30-50%-extended version to ISBRA'23 special issues of:
  • IEEE/ACM Transactions on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics
  • IEEE Transactions on NanoBioscience
  • Journal of Computational Biology

Track 2

Submit up to 12 pages in LNCS format for:

  • Presentation at the conference
  • invitation to submit a full-length paper to one of the following journals:
  • BMC Bioinformatics
  • BMC Genomics
